Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

A Common Service Centre (CSC) is a government-backed initiative to provide various digital services to citizens in rural and remote areas. It acts as a one-stop-shop for G2C (Government to Citizen) and B2C (Business to Citizen) services.

Our center is open from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday to Saturday. We are closed on Sundays and public holidays.

We accept cash, UPI (like Google Pay, PhonePe), and other digital payment methods. For specific services, payment might be accepted through the official government portal, which we will assist you with.

No appointment is necessary. You can walk in during our business hours for any service. However, for specialized services, you can call us in advance to ensure a smooth process.

Aadhaar & PAN Services

For most Aadhaar updates (like address or name), you will need a valid Proof of Identity (POI) and Proof of Address (POA) document. For mobile number updates, no documents are required, but your biometric authentication is necessary.

Typically, an e-PAN card is generated and sent to your email within a few hours to 2-3 days. The physical PAN card is delivered to your address by post within 15-20 working days.
