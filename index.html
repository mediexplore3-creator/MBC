What is a Common Service Centre (CSC)?

The Common Service Centre (CSC) is a government-approved digital access point for delivering essential public utility services, social welfare schemes, healthcare, financial, education, and agriculture services. Our goal is to provide these e-services to citizens in rural and remote areas, bridging the digital divide and fostering a digitally inclusive society.
